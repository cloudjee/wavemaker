Palo Alto Campus Update
Published by on Apr 28, 2011
Were planning for future growth, and VMware is in discussions to acquire additional space to expand our campus in the Stanford Research Park in Palo Alto.  Assuming the deal closes, well be able to bring all of our Palo Alto employees together onto a spectacular site thats contiguous with our Promontory buildings.
 
This is exciting, but nothing is final yet and the agreement is still confidential. Please dont discuss this outside of VMware. Lets let PR do their jobs, so be sure to send questions by analysts and media to our PR department.
 
We know that you have questions as well, and lots more information is coming hopefully in June.
 
The things we do to expand parking!  ;-)
 
Mark
